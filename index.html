Hello, and welcome to my page!

I'm an Auxiliary Researcher at Centro de Investigação em Biodiversidade e Recursos Genéticos (CIBIO-InBio).

I came face to face with a wild female boar with three piglets and got away with only a scratch. I got into Academia at a younger age than most people are allowed in. I fought hordes of ticks in Coruche and didn't get Lyme's disease. I watched the sunset from within a cork oak tree, crossed paths with the same grumpy fox several times, and watched small stone martens play hide and seek. 

My name is Filipe. You may have heard of me.

If the above paragraphs sound familiar, that's because you have excellent taste in books.
